设 $f(x, y)=-y+x^2+xy+x^4+y^4$.

Prob 1. 以 $(1, 1)$ 为初值, 用梯度下降法求 $f$ 最小值的近似值, 保留三位小数.

$f_x = 4x^3 + 2x + y$, $f_y = 4y^3 + x - 1$.

故 $\nabla f = (4x^3+2x+y, 4y^3+x-1)$, Hesse 矩阵为 $\left[\begin{matrix}12x^2+2 & 1 \\ 1 & 12y^2\end{matrix}\right]$.

最优步长为 $\frac{\lVert \nabla f \rVert^2}{\nabla f^t H \nabla f}$.

通过编写程序计算得到

$f(1, 1) = 3$.
$f(0.512848, 0.721627) = 0.251823$.
$f(0.136618, 0.554479) = -0.365191$.
$f(-0.310065, 0.651229) = -0.567909$.
$f(-0.294078, 0.688472) = -0.572305$.
$f(-0.293816, 0.686389) = -0.572317$.
