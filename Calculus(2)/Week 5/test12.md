Prob 1. 计算 $\int_{-\frac{\pi}{2}}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \int_1^2 e^y \sin(\frac{x}{y}) \text{d}y\text{d}x$.

$$
\begin{aligned}
\int_{-\frac{\pi}{2}}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \int_1^2 e^y \sin(\frac{x}{y}) \text{d}y\text{d}x &= \int_1^2 e^y \int_{-\frac{\pi}{2}}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \sin(\frac{x}{y}) \text{d}x\text{d}y
\\
&= 0.
\end{aligned}
$$
