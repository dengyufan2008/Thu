解方程.

Prob 1. $xy''+(4x-2)y'-4y=0$, $y(0)=1$.

$\mathscr{L}[xy''] = \int_0^\infty e^{-tx}xy''(x) \text{d}x = -\frac{\text{d}}{\text{d}t} \int_0^\infty e^{-tx}y''(x) \text{d}x = -\frac{\text{d}}{\text{d}t} (t(tY-1)-y'(0)) = -\frac{\text{d}}{\text{d}t} (t^2Y-t-y'(0)) = -2tY - t^2Y' + 1$.

$\mathscr{L}[(4x-2)y'] = -4\frac{\text{d}}{\text{d}t} (tY-1) - 2(tY-1) = -(4+2t)Y - 4tY' + 2$.

故 $-(4+4t)Y - (t^2+4t)Y' + 3 = 0$. 谁爱解谁解😡
